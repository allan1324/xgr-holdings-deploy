WELCOME

From Curator to Creator

Charlie’s Winery, a proud and dynamic part of XGR Holdings, stands as a testament to entrepreneurial vision and a deep passion for fine spirits. Our journey began not in the fields of distillation, but as a discerning retail outlet, curating the best beverages for a growing market.

That experience on the front lines of consumer preference ignited a new ambition: to craft our own legacy. Today, Charlie’s Winery is dedicated to creating distinctive, high-quality spirits that capture the essence of tradition while embracing innovative craftsmanship.

FROM CURATORTO CREATOR

OUR FOUNDATION

A Taste for Excellence

Our foundation is built on deep market insight, an unyielding pursuit of quality, and the strategic backing of XGR Holdings. This combination blends the agility of a specialized beverage producer with the stability and global reach of a diversified holding company.

OUR PURPOSE

Elevating the Spirit Experience

We exist to elevate the spirit experience, transforming raw ingredients into liquid artistry. Our purpose is to craft, distribute, and market exceptional beverage brands that delight consumers, celebrate craftsmanship, and create lasting value for our stakeholders.
